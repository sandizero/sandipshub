Amazon PPC Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive view of Amazon PPC campaign performance, enabling data-driven optimization strategies.

Overview

This project involved developing a sophisticated Power BI dashboard to analyze and visualize Amazon PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising data. The goal was to transform raw, complex advertising metrics into clear, actionable insights, allowing stakeholders to quickly understand campaign performance, identify optimization opportunities, and ultimately improve Ad ROAS (Return on Ad Spend).

The dashboard integrates data from Amazon Seller Central, providing real-time tracking of key performance indicators (KPIs) such as ad spend, revenue, ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales), TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales), clicks, impressions, and conversions.

Problem & Solution

Problem: The client was struggling to efficiently monitor and optimize their Amazon PPC campaigns. Data was fragmented, requiring manual aggregation from various reports, leading to delayed insights and missed opportunities for cost reduction or performance improvement.

Solution: I designed and implemented a robust ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) process to pull daily PPC data automatically. This data was then cleaned, transformed, and loaded into Power BI. The dashboard provides a single source of truth, featuring interactive filters, drill-down capabilities, and visualizations that highlight:

  • Overall campaign performance trends.
  • Performance by campaign, with ACOS ascending and descending order.
  • Perticuler campain performece in varrious placement with drill-down.
  • Campaign that have no sales.
  • Placement wise Campaign analysis.
  • Seasonal trends and campaign seasonality.

Technologies Used

  • Power BI: For interactive dashboard design and data visualization.
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): For creating custom measures and calculated columns.
  • Power Query: For data extraction, transformation, and loading.
  • SQL: (If applicable, e.g., for initial data staging or specific data pulls) Used for structuring and querying advertising data.
  • Google Sheet: For initial data review and validation.
  • Amazon Seller Central: Source of raw PPC data.

Results & Impact

The implementation of this dashboard led to significant improvements for the client:

  • 20% Increase in Ad ROAS: By identifying underperforming keywords and optimizing bids based on data-driven insights.
  • Reduced Manual Reporting Time: Automation of data aggregation saved approximately 10 hours per week of manual effort.
  • Improved Decision-Making: Stakeholders gained immediate access to critical performance metrics, enabling faster and more informed strategic adjustments to ad campaigns.
  • Enhanced Accountability: Clear visualization of performance metrics fostered better accountability among the marketing team.
